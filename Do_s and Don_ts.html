Do's and Don'ts

What should we do before, during and after the vaccination.

What you should to do and don't

Before the Vaccination

You should do

  • Eat well
  • Stay hydrated
  • In case a person has allergies to medication, or drugs please get prescirbed from your doctor
  • Wear mask to the center

You should avoid

  • Taking steroids
  • receiving a COVID-19 vaccine if you are sick or experiencing symptoms of COVID-19
  • going empty stomach
  • Taking pain Medicine
  • Avoid alcohol
  • Don't plan on immediately leaving
  • To schedule Other Vaccines the Same Day
What you should to do and don't

During the Vaccination

You should do

  • Consult your doctor before the vaccination if you are on steroids or blood thinners
  • wearing masks, regularly washing, or sanitizing your hands, maintaining physical distance, avoid crowded places and avoid touching surfaces.
  • Apply a clean, cool, and wet cloth (or some ice) over the arm after the vaccination to reduce the pain.
  • Wear mask to the center

You should avoid

  • Delay consulting a doctor if you experience COVID-19 symptoms
  • Avoid alcohol and tobacco
  • Avoid strenuous physical activity
  • Do not miss other necessary vaccines
